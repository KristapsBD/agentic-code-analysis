## Findings

### 1. access_control

- **Severity:** CRITICAL
- **Location:** `SplitPoolAmountFrom() at line 626`
- **Confidence:** HIGH

**Description:**

The SplitPoolAmountFrom function incorrectly updates the allowance of the recipient (\_Address) instead of the spender (msg.sender). This allows a spender with a non-zero allowance to repeatedly split a pool and drain its tokens without their allowance being deducted, effectively bypassing the authorization limit set by the pool owner.

**Recommendation:**

In the `SplitPoolAmountFrom` function, change line 626 from `pool.Allowance[_Address] = _NewAmount;` to `pool.Allowance[msg.sender] = _NewAmount;` to ensure the spender's allowance is correctly decremented.
